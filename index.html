How it works

Simple as it's always been.

1

Sign up

Create your account and tell us where to deliver. It only takes a couple of minutes. Sign up here.

2

Set up a standing order

Set up a standing order so we know what to keep on the trucks. You are not obligated to take it every week. For example, it might be two half gallons of whole milk, a quart of chocolate, and a dozen eggs. You can add to it or change it right up until the night before delivery.

3

We'll remind you

Every week we'll send a reminder email the day before delivery, along with any specials that are available. Just log in online, or use the app if that's easier, to make changes or skip a delivery.

4

Leave the empties out

We'll provide you with a Rise 'n Shine milkbox while you're a customer. Just rinse your bottles once they're empty and place them back in the milkbox. There's a $1.50 deposit on each bottle, and you'll see a credit on your bill for each bottle you return.

5

You're always in charge

Skip a week, change your order, or stop entirely. You can do it all from your account in under a minute. No runaround, no phone calls.

6

No surprises

You'll see the same milkman every week, someone you'll come to recognize who knows your standing order. No surprises, no rotating drivers, just exactly what you ordered.

That's genuinely it. No fuss at all. 🥛
